GOJO SATORU


Gojo Satoru was born on December 7, 1989, making him 28 years old in
the current timeline of Jujutsu Kaisen.012 After his birth, the balance
of power shifted, and he had a bounty put on his head and was targeted
by bounty hunters. During his second year as a student, Gojo along with
his classmates, Suguru Geto and Shoko Ieiri, went to check up on Mei Mei
and Utahime after not hearing from them in two days.2 Gojo was born as a
member of the Gojo family, one of the biggest three Jujutsu sorcerer
families. There is no information about the members of the Gojo family
other than Satoru
